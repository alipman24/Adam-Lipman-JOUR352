The Rise of the Left Handed Sweeper


MLB pitching's newest trend


By Adam Lipman


November 24, 2025


Left handed pitchers are on the rise again after Detroit Tigers starter Tarik Skubal and Boston Red Sox pitcher Garrett Crochet finished in the top two for American League Cy Young Voting.

Those lefties, alongside the Philadelphia Phillies' Jesus Luzardo, ranked atop the 2025 MLB strikeout leaderboard and each added a new pitch to their arsenal in recent years.

The sweeper, a modified version of the slider, adds more horizontal movement since launching into the mainstream back in late 2022. The question is: can those pitchers credit this new addition for their elite performances?
